Post Incident Review Training

A guide to learning from surprises


Welcome to Post Incident Review Training!
The increasing complexity of modern software services makes it highly likely that everyone working in IT will occasionally have to respond to a system failure.
A Post Incident Review is your opportunity to learn from these situations and feed that knowledge back into your organisation.
This course will enable you to lead your own Post Incident Reviews.
Unit one contains two lessons that introduce the foundational elements of successful Post Incident Reviews.
Unit two contains two lessons that provide a guide to conducting successful Post Incident Reviews.

Infusing your organisation with the knowledge gained from reviewing incidents requires a significant amount of time and energy—thank you for making that investment!

  • Examples of IT incidents
  • Approaches to post incident reviews
  • The purpose of a post incident review
  • Building a learning culture with language
  • How blame is assigned
  • How expertise is shared
