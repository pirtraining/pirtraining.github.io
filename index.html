Welcome to Post Incident Review Training!
If you build or support modern software services, this training is for you.

Everyone working on these services occasionally has to respond to an unexpected failure. Surprise!
A Post Incident Review is your opportunity to learn from these situations and feed that knowledge into your organisation.

• Sharing a deeper knowledge of the system empowers engineers to make their jobs easier.
• Commitment to continuous improvement helps managers build trust and resilience among teams.
• Producing thorough records of incidents enables organisations to discover patterns that will reduce the risk of future incidents.

Course organisation +

The content of this course is organised into two parts:
• Part one contains two lessons introducing the foundational elements of successful Post Incident Reviews.
• Part two contains two lessons providing a guide to conducting successful Post Incident Reviews.

Start with part one if you are new to Post Incident Reviews.
If you are already familiar with Post Incident Reviews, but it’s been a while, then part two is for you!
