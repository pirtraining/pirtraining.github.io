You have reached the end of this lesson on facilitation, and the end of this Post Incident Review Training course!


In this lesson you learned some best practices for running a fruitful PIR meeting for incident participants.

Hopefully this course has given you enough context and pointers to make you feel comfortable leading your own PIR.

That said, reviewing incidents is a skill sharpened by experience and the best way to gain that experience is to get out there and put these principles into practice. Happy reviewing!
