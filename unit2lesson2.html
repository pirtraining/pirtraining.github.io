Facilitating Post Incident Reviews

“By supporting everyone to do their best thinking, a facilitator enables group members to search for inclusive solutions and build sustainable agreements.”


—Kaner et al. (2007, p.32)

Introduction

In part one, you learned that the key role in a PIR is that of PIR leader. A PIR leader has two core responsibilities:

  1. To prepare a document that captures the details of the PIR—the PIR report.
  2. To facilitate a group discussion between those closest to the event—the PIR meeting.


This lesson is about facilitation and will focus on the second responsibility, which is to navigate an effective PIR meeting.



Lesson Objectives

After studying this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Structure a practical PIR meeting.
  • Effectively follow up on the findings of the PIR meeting.
