Facilitating Post Incident Reviews

“By supporting everyone to do their best thinking, a facilitator enables group members to search for inclusive solutions and build sustainable agreements.”


—Kaner et al. (2007, p.32)

Introduction

In the previous lesson, we learned how to prepare for a successful PIR.
By following this guidance, you should be familiar with a document that captures the details of the PIR—the PIR report.

This lesson is about facilitation and will focus on using this document to facilitate a group discussion between those close to the event—the PIR meeting.



Lesson Objectives

After studying this lesson and the relevant recommended readings, students should be able to:

  • Recognise the importance of setting a clear agenda for a PIR meeting.
  • Identify the core segments of a PIR meeting (agenda then timeline then discuss).
  • Identify the importance of assigning owners to action items.
